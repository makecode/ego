Game description

Play one of the most popular and challenging Zelda games of all time! Repeat the three-day cycle over and over again in The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask! This action-adventure video game was released back in 2000 and is the sixth main installment of the Legend of Zelda main series. The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask is very popular as it features a darker theme with a more grim story, graphics, events, and plot. The game is set in a place called Termina, which is an alternate version of the Hyrule.
